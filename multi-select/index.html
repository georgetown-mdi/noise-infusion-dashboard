About this view

Earnings are matched via UI wage records. Participants must have completed at least one quarter of employment to be included.

Click the Demographics tab to view participant characteristics — and see what happens when demographic filters are enabled.

What does ~ mean? The tilde prefix signals deliberate perturbation — not measurement uncertainty. The agency knows the exact value but adds calibrated noise before publishing. An adversary who tries to back-calculate a hidden figure (e.g. Total earnings − visible subgroups) recovers a noisy result, not the true count. Unlike suppression, every cell is still published — there's no missing data to signal that a subgroup is small.
Adding these multi-select filters allows users to drill into subgroups — reducing the matched sample size below safe thresholds.
